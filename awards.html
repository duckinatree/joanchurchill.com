Awards

Churchill is the recipient of the following awards amongst others:

British Academy Award (BAFTA)

Prix Italia

DuPont Columbia Award for Outstanding Journalism

IDA Award for Outstanding Achievement in Documentary Cinematography

California State Bar Award for Distinguished Reporting

Sundance, Chicago & Tribeca Festivals’ Prizes

Hague Peace Prize

Amnesty International Doen Award

Women in Film’s Kodak Vision Award/Documentary

Churchill is the first documentary cinematographer to be accepted into the American Society of Cinematographers (A.S.C.)
& one of the few women

Joan Churchill is an independent film-maker and camera woman. She lives and works in Los Angeles California. She is
currently seeking funding for a documentary on her long-time compatriot and award winning documentarian Haskell Wexler.
She is also available for speaking engagements. Get in touch.
